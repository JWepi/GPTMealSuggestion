Ingredients:
- 4 chicken breasts
- Salt and pepper
- 120g flour
- 2 eggs, lightly beaten
- 200g breadcrumbs
- Oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Flatten chicken breasts.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Coat in flour.
4. Dip in beaten eggs.
5. Dredge in breadcrumbs.
6. Heat oil in pan.
7. Fry until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.

Serve with desired accompaniments (e.g., lemon wedge, parsley).
