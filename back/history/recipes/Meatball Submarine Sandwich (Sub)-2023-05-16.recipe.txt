Meatball Submarine Sandwich (Sub)

Ingredients:
500g ground meat
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
50g breadcrumbs
1 egg
Salt and pepper
Olive oil
400g marinara sauce
4 sub rolls
200g mozzarella cheese

Instructions:
1. Mix ground meat, chopped onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs, egg, salt and pepper.
2. Form mixture into meatballs.
3. Cook meatballs in olive oil until browned.
4. Add marinara sauce to pan; simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Slice sub rolls; toast lightly.
6. Place cooked meatballs on toasted rolls.
7. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
8. Broil sandwiches until cheese melts and browns slightly.
9. Serve hot
