Ingredients:
200g ramen noodles
1L vegetable broth 
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp miso paste
100g bok choy
50g sliced mushrooms
2 green onions

Instructions:
1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
2. Heat vegetable broth in a pot, add soy sauce and miso paste.
3. Add bok choy and mushrooms, cook for 5 minutes.
4. Divide cooked noodles between bowls.
5. Pour hot broth with vegetables over noodles.
6. Garnish with sliced green onions, serve immediately.
