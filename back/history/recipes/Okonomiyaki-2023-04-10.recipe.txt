Ingredients:
100g flour
160ml water
2 eggs
200g cabbage
50g green onions
100g pork belly

Instructions:
1. Mix flour and water.
2. Add eggs, mix well.
3. Stir in cabbage, green onions.
4. Heat pan, add oil.
5. Pour batter onto pan, shape into a circle.
6. Top with pork belly slices.
7. Cook 3-5 minutes each side or until golden brown and cooked through.
