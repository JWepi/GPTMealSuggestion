Oyakodon

Ingredients:
- 1 chicken thigh
- 1 onion
- 2 eggs
- 100ml dashi stock
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp sugar

Instructions:
1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
2. Thinly slice onion.
3. Beat eggs in a bowl.
4. In a pan, combine dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar.
5. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
6. Add onions and cook until softened.
7. Add chicken and cook until cooked through.
8. Pour beaten eggs over the mixture in the pan.
9. Cover and cook on low heat for about two minutes or until eggs are set but still slightly runny.
10. Serve over steamed rice in a bowl.
