Ingredients:

400 g mushrooms
1 onion
2 garlic cloves
300 ml vegetable broth
200 ml cream
8 thin beef slices
Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Chop mushrooms, onion, and garlic.
2. Saut√© onions and garlic in butter until soft.
3. Add mushrooms, cook for 5 minutes.
4. Stir in half of the vegetable broth.
5. Simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Spread mushroom mixture on beef slices.
7. Roll up each slice tightly, secure with toothpicks.
8. Brown rouladen in a pan with oil.
9. Pour remaining vegetable broth into pan.
10. Cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
11.Remove rouladen from pan, set aside.
12.Add cream to the liquid in the pan.
13.Bring to boil, thicken if desired with cornstarch or flour slurry.
14.Season sauce with salt and pepper.
15.Serve rouladen topped with creamy mushroom sauce.
