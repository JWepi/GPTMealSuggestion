French Onion Soup

Ingredients:
1 kg onions
50 g butter
2 tbsp flour
200 ml white wine
1.5 L beef broth
Baguette slices
Grated Gruy√®re cheese

Instructions:
1. Thinly slice onions.
2. Melt butter in large pot, cook onions until caramelized.
3. Stir in flour, cook for 2 minutes.
4. Add wine and beef broth, bring to boil.
5. Lower heat, simmer for 30 minutes.
6. Preheat oven broiler/grill.
7. Toast baguette slices on both sides.
8. Ladle soup into ovenproof bowls, top with toasted bread and cheese.
9. Broil until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Serve hot and enjoy!
