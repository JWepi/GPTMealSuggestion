Recipe for 'Chicken Enchiladas'

Ingredients:
500g chicken
15ml oil
200g onions
1 garlic clove
100g tomatoes
3 green chilies
8 tortillas
300g cheese

Instructions:
1. Cook chicken.
2. Heat oil.
3. Sauté onions, garlic, tomatoes, chilies.
4. Shred chicken, add to mix.
5. Fill tortillas with mixture and cheese.
6. Roll and place in baking dish.
7. Top with remaining cheese.
8. Bake at 180°C for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
