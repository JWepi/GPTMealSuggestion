Onigiri

Ingredients:
300g sushi rice
360ml water
Salt
Nori seaweed sheets

Instructions:
1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear.
2. Cook rice with water in a rice cooker or pot.
3. Allow cooked rice to cool slightly.
4. Wet hands, add salt and form small balls or triangles of rice.
5. Wrap Onigiri with Nori seaweed sheet.
6. Serve and enjoy!
