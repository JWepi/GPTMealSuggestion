Recipe for 'Stuffed Poblano Peppers'

Ingredients:
4 poblano peppers
200g ground beef
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
100g cooked rice
50g corn kernels
50g black beans
1 tomato
100g shredded cheese
2 tbsp oil

Instructions:
1. Roast poblanos, peel and deseed.
2. Brown ground beef in oil.
3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until soft.
4. Mix in cooked rice, corn, black beans, and diced tomato.
5. Stuff poblanos with mixture.
6. Top with cheese and bake at 180Â°C for 20 minutes.
