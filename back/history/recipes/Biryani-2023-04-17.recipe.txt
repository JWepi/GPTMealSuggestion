Recipe for 'Biryani'

Ingredients:
500g basmati rice
1 kg chicken
200g yogurt
2 onions
3 tomatoes
4 green chilies
1/4 cup mint leaves
1/4 cup coriander leaves
2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
2 tbsp Biryani masala powder
1 tsp red chili powder 
1/2 tsp turmeric powder 
4 cloves, 4 cardamom, 2 bay leaves, 1 cinnamon stick, 1 star anise 
5 cups water

Instructions:
Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, Biryani masala powder.
Fry sliced onions until golden brown.
Add marinated chicken to fried onions. Cook for few minutes.
Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies. Cook until tender.
Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
Boil water with whole spices (cloves, cardamom, bay leaves).
Drain soaked rice and add it to boiling water. Cook until half done.
Layer cooked rice over the cooked chicken mixture in a deep pan or pot.
Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves on top of the rice layer.
Cover tightly and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes.
