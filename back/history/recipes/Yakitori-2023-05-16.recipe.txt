Yakitori

Ingredients: 
- 500g boneless chicken thighs
- 1 bunch green onions
- 100ml soy sauce
- 100ml mirin
- 50ml sake
- 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions:
1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
2. Cut green onions into 3cm lengths.
3. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar in a saucepan.
4. Bring to boil and simmer until slightly thickened.
5. Skewer chicken and green onion alternately.
6. Grill skewers on medium heat, turning occasionally.
7. Baste with sauce during grilling until cooked through.

Serve hot with extra yakitori sauce if desired.
